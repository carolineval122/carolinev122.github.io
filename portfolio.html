Little Us WordPress Site Development

UI/UX Design, WordPress

This high-fidelity website is designed specifically for the up and coming rock band Little Us. Little Us website information had not been updated since 2019, runs incredibly slow, and has an outdated theme. Since their website was previously designed on WordPress, I optimized the layout and infromation architecture of the site. Additionally, I implemented plugins to give more functionality and animation to the page. The website has a simple interface with navigation to upcoming shows, music videos, and the latest released music. Additionally, the site showcases the band members, allows users to purchase merchandise, includes forms to contact the band, and a button to subscribe to their newsletter. This website design directly reflects the band's personality and music style, which can be seen in the bold color choices against a dark background. Use the link to preview the prototype.
