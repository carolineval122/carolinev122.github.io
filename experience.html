All Occassions LLC

Website Management

TFor All Occassions LLC, I focused on enhancing the current site while also improving the user experience. My research and analysis of the site's accessibility led to the discovery of a lack of alt text and other features that hinder the site from being reached by search crawlers. Thus, I reconstructed the information architecture to enhance usability and implemented SEO techniques to increase both organic website traffic and search rankings. Overall, my work greatly enhanced the brand's online presence and image. With a redesigned website, customers will enjoy shopping for products and this website can actually reach potential customers.

The Moon Lady Company

Art Installation

Working in a group of 5, we were tasked with creating an installation to present in part of the UCF Celebrates the Arts "Visual Tales" exhibition. Hand-built from the ground up, we created 2D vectors using tools such as Adobe Illustrator to then laser cut and hand paint individual wood pieces. As my first collaboration project, I gained effective communication skills and used constructive feedback from team members to improve throughout the process. Delegating tasks and ensuring we met our deadlines were crucial to creating a successful piece. Most importantly, working in a team helped me build my relationships and become adaptable to solving challenges that arose.
